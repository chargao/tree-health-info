About the Project

We are an international group, primarily based in California, Texas, and Germany. What unites us is our shared love for nature, our ecosystems, and a deep curiosity about the processes that occur within them.

Our mission is to help people better understand and observe the natural processes in their immediate surroundings by providing them with educational resources and opportunities, such as informational materials and workshops.

At the same time, we aim to act as a connector, bringing together groups that have already taken action and are actively contributing to environmental initiatives.

Climatebase

Climatebase is a leading platform dedicated to mobilizing talent for climate solutions. Since its launch in July 2020, it has become the world's foremost platform for climate careers, assisting hundreds of thousands of individuals in discovering job opportunities at over 3,000 organizations.

The platform offers a comprehensive climate jobs portal, the Climatebase Fellowship—a 12-week online program designed to advance careers in climate—and the Climatebase Weekly newsletter, which reaches over 100,000 readers.

By providing these resources, Climatebase aims to accelerate climate solutions by building products, programs, and a community to grow the climate workforce and support the hiring needs of thousands of organizations in the sector.
